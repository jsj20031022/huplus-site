About HuPlus

Asia's most curated AI tools directory

Our mission: Help Asia's 4 billion people find and use AI tools that actually matter — without the noise.

What we do

HuPlus is a free, independently curated directory of the world's best AI tools, reviewed and categorized for Asian creators, developers, marketers and entrepreneurs.

We test tools. We read the docs. We talk to real users. Then we distill it all into clear, honest listings so you can make smart decisions fast.

Why "HuPlus"?

Hub of Plus-value tools. Every listing on this site earns its spot by delivering genuine value — free or paid, indie or enterprise, new or established.

How we make money

We keep this site free through two transparent means:

  • Affiliate commissions — Some links are affiliate links. If you click and buy/sign up, we may earn a small commission at no cost to you. We only link to tools we'd genuinely recommend.
  • Display advertising — We run Google AdSense ads to offset hosting and curation costs.

We never accept payment to feature or positively review a tool. Our editorial independence is non-negotiable.
